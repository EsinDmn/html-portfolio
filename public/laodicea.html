Welcome to Laodicea

Laodicea was the most important cultural, art, trace, and sports center in antiquity. The city was designed with the Hippodamian grid plan. Sadly, In the 5th century A.D., the city was demolished by a great earthquake. It was very popular with the trade of its wool, textile, and marble because Laodiceans were best at producing textile products.
